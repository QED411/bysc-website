Travel Soccer (WYSL)

We employ a third-party training organization to run the tryout process. We primarily use those results, supplemented by coach and team trainer evaluations.

We anticipate 1 team for each gender in each age group from U-10 through U-14.

Per US Soccer Federation guidelines, teams are formed based on calendar year birthdates, regardless of grade level.
