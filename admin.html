Getting Started

Admin Instructions

1

Google Drive Access

All shared club files (budgets, meeting minutes, rosters, templates, etc.) are stored in our Google Drive. Request access from the current Board President if you haven't been added.

Open BYSC Google Drive
2

AYSO Region 202 Admin

Register as a volunteer at ayso202.org. Complete your Safe Haven certification and any required background checks through AYSO's system. Coordinate with the Regional Commissioner for admin-level access.

3

WYSL (Travel Soccer) Admin

Travel program coordination goes through the Westchester Youth Soccer League. Login credentials for GotSport are managed by the Travel Director. Contact them for roster management, scheduling, and match card access.

4

Website Management

This website is hosted as a static site. To make updates, edit the files in the BYSC Website folder and redeploy. The domain briarcliffsoccer.org is registered through GoDaddy. Contact the webmaster for DNS or hosting changes.

5

Financial & Banking

BYSC operates as a 501(c)(3) non-profit. The Treasurer manages the club bank account and handles all registration fee collection, trainer payments, and league dues. Refer to the incorporation documents below for our EIN and tax-exempt details.

6

Seasonal Checklist

  • Open registration on ayso202.org (typically April/May)
  • Secure professional trainers for the season
  • Confirm field permits with the Village/School District
  • Recruit volunteer coaches (email blast + website)
  • Run travel tryouts in June (hire 3rd-party evaluator)
  • Balance AYSO team rosters by rising grade
  • Distribute uniforms and game schedules
  • Verify insurance certificates are current
  • Update website with current season info

Need a Certificate of Insurance (COI)?

Schools and municipal facilities often require a COI naming them as an additional insured. Contact AYSO Region 202 to request a certificate with the specific entity listed. Typical turnaround is 5–7 business days.
